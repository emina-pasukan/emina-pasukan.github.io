Princess Diana

The Princess of Wales but also "The Queen of people's hearts..." And honestly, I can't think of a better title for her. A true example of grace, emapthy, humanity and kindness.

A woman,a mother, a princess who didn't accept the world around her the way it was. And she could. She didn't have t care about people behind the walls of Buckingham palace. Buy yet, she did. She believed she can make a difference. To me she is an example of a true leader.

Here is also why she still ispires me:

One of her messages for the world was:“It really doesn’t matter what you wear or how you do your hair,” said Diana. “What matters is who’s inside the clothes and their heart.” And I couldn't agree with her more.
