About Us

Swimming Club Bosna was founded back in 1960, when it was just a group of enthusiasts who shared love and passion for swimming. First activities as aregistered club were recorded in January, 1966. The club was officialy registered on October 1st, 1981 with the club's first president Refik Kravic. Today, many years after that, swimming club Bosna is still home of true champions where they share love for sport, their teammates and where they make unforgettable memories, as well as lifelong friendships.

Our Values

  • Tradition- One of the oldest swimming clubs in the country with over 40 yeras long tradition
  • Community-Community is always on top of the list of our priorities and we try to show our support during our competitions
  • Integrity

Why choose "Bosna" as your team?

With us you will have an opportunity to practice with team who has long-lasting tradition, experienced coaching staff, to proudly represent your community and your country.
